# Delivery Roadmap Mapping

## Weeks 1-4

- Workspace + service skeletons.
- Shared contracts and event schema.
- Risk engine and preset management.
- Gateway endpoint surface and dashboard shell.

## Weeks 5-8

- Polymarket adapter implementation.
- Approval queue and live-safe execution flow.
- Position and PnL state.
- Kill switch and incident hooks.

## Weeks 9-14

- Multi-source features.
- Model router with Claude/OpenAI/OpenClow.
- Confidence and evaluation jobs.

## Weeks 15-20

- Multi-venue adapter framework.
- Replay/backfill and resilience hardening.

## Weeks 21-26

- RBAC, MFA, secret rotation.
- Immutable decision ledger.
- DR drills and production certification.
